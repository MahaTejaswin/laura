Our Story

A Family Tradition Since 1952

Laura was founded by Nonna Laura Rossi, who brought her family's secret recipes from Tuscany. For over 70 years, we've been serving traditional Italian cuisine made with love.

Our kitchen is led by Chef Marco Rossi, trained in the finest restaurants of Rome and Florence.
